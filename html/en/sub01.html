Product

Swingsaver

BirdieYa! Network is a proprietary communication system developed to connect all facilities and infrastructures within a golf course under one unified platform.
Without external networks or additional communication costs, it ensures stable and efficient data transmission through its own network and can be flexibly expanded to various operational applications.

  • Scalability & Compatibility
  • Certified in Korea, Japan, and the U.S.
  • 100% compatibility with all devices and sensors
  • Scalability & Operational Scope
  • Expandable for game, facility, and course management
  • Wide coverage and zero additional communication cost

  • ADAS Device for Safety (Advanced Driver Assistance)

    • Compatible with all cart types — no structural modification required
    • Autonomously prevents collisions without external intervention
    • Automatically slows down or stops in hazardous zones
    • Applies AI object recognition & driving prediction algorithms

  • App for Convenience (AI Caddie & AI Remote)

    • Supports both smartphone and smartwatch
    • Provides real-time round info (distance, pin location, direction, etc.)
    • Auto-follow and cart call features available
    • Replaces traditional rangefinders and remotes

  • Hole Signal Light Device

    • Prevents ball-strike accidents
    • Warns when the front group is within striking range
    • Effective at intersections, blind holes, and doglegs
    • Guides green direction during fog

  • AI Marshal App

    • App for course marshal management
    • Real-time monitoring of cart flow
    • Real-time tracking of cart status and movement
    • Can replace traditional cart control systems

  • Remote Control Device

    • Controls pumps, ponds, water tanks, fountains, septic systems, etc.
    • Monitors and controls facility status & enables auto data collection
    • Generates RAW data for AI-based autonomous operation
    • Addresses climate change, labor shortages, and cost reduction

  • Control Center Application

    • Provides full visibility of all facility operations at a glance
    • Monitors and remotely controls individual facilities
    • Displays operating hours, usage data, and other insights
    • Mobile-based management (PC compatible)

  • Automatic Water Level Sensor

    • Automatically detects water levels in ponds and water tanks
    • Tracks current capacity and usage volume over time
    • Linked with pumps and W/T for autonomous water supply control
    • Automated sprinkler and water tank control

  • Irrigation Control Example

    • Mobile-based work management
    • Autonomous operation system between devices

  • Soil Information Detection & Data Sharing

    • Applicable to tee boxes, fairways, and greens
    • Automatically detects soil pH, temperature, and humidity
    • Generates RAW data for AI analysis
    • Supports climate and labor adaptability with cost reduction

  • Work Vehicle Management

    • Track work vehicle history
    • Manage vehicle maintenance
    • Monitor fertilizer and chemical routes

  • Geofencing Monitoring

    • Displays pest and repair zones using GIS
    • Restricts unauthorized cart entry
    • Visualizes any required field data

  • Work Report, Forecast & Alerts

    • Daily work orders, results, and shared records
    • Dryness and pest alerts
    • Fertilizer & chemical management
