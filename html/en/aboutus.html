What’s BirdieYa! System?

BirdieYa! System is a smart golf course management platform built on our proprietary network (N/W) and AIoT (Artificial Intelligence + IoT) technology. It is designed to automatically and autonomously manage major assets on the golf course such as carts, turf, and facilities—all within a single platform and without additional communication costs.
This system provides practical benefits by preventing safety accidents, responding to climate change, solving workforce shortages, and reducing operational costs—thereby improving work efficiency and profitability for golf courses.
BirdieYa! System continues to evolve to address challenges not only in Korea but also in the global golf course market, expanding its technological capabilities and reaching toward the world stage.

02

BirdieYa! ADAS Module

BirdieYa! ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance System) is an AI-based advanced golf cart driver assistance system developed by Swingsaver.
It can be easily installed on existing carts without structural modifications, minimizing human intervention while providing both accident prevention and convenience.
This on-device AI solution is the only true innovation for the era of self-rounding.
