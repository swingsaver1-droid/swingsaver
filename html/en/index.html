BirdieYa!’s Innovative AIoT Technology

  • Network Device

    - Core product realizing our proprietary AIoT System
    - Certified in Korea, Japan, and the USA
    - Enables unified management using data connectivity
    - Addresses safety, labor shortages, cost reduction, and climate change

  • ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance) Device

    - Compatible with all existing carts without structural modification
    - Transforms into an on-device AI cart for safety and convenience
    - Prevents collisions autonomously
    - Slows or stops in danger zones to prevent accidents

  • Hole Signal Light (IoT Edge Device)

    - Lamp warning when front group is in striking range
    - Used for blind intersections
    - Ideal for blind or dogleg holes
    - Can indicate green direction during fog

  • Remote Control Device (IoT Edge Device)

    - Connects pumps, ponds, water tanks, fountains, septic systems, and more
    - Enables monitoring, control, and auto data collection
    - Performs autonomous, intelligent diagnostics and early warnings
    - Reduces management costs and manpower needs
